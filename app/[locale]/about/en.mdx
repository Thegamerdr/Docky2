# About PerfumeVS

Welcome to PerfumeVS, your ultimate destination for perfume comparison and discovery.

## Our Mission

We believe that finding your perfect scent shouldn't be complicated. Our platform helps you:

- Compare perfumes side by side
- Discover similar fragrances at different price points
- Make informed decisions about your purchases

## How It Works

1. Search for your favorite perfumes
2. Compare their notes, longevity, and prices
3. Find similar fragrances that match your preferences
4. Save your favorites to your wishlist
